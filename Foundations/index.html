Foundations

This is the Odin Project(OP)'s required first course. A lot like remedial math or 'bonehead English' as we called it in my High School. Vo-Ag (Vocational Agriculture) was the largest school department, and the largest school club was the FFA (Future Farmers of America). I really wanted to take the Vo-Ag Welding and Auto Mechanics classes, but you had to be part of the program to take them. So I took a correspondence course in Radio and Television Repair instead. Vacuum tubes are pretty simple but calculations by hand (pre handheld calculator days) were a real pain.

That class probably fed me for more years of my life than any other single influence.

That and typing. I took typing because of an intense adolescent crush on the typing teacher who was first year out of the teacher's college, and to my young eyes the prettiest thing I'd ever seen. So to keep from her scorn, I worked very hard at forcing my fumbling fingers up to 60 words per minute with no errors. I think that was the peak of my typing speed.
